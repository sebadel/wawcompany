Coaching is the process of supporting an individual or a group towards a phase of change or development.

Anyone who wants to bring their team or their personal performance to the next level would find in coaching a structured way to achieve their objectives.

It is also possible to facilitate teamwork towards finding solutions to specific company matters.

As coaching is a widely used technique, the industry is now professionalized by the ICF (International Coaching Federation), which recognizes educated and experienced coaches in the world.
